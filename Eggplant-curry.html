Eggplant Curry Recipe

Ingredients:
  • Eggplant
  • Curry powder
  • Onion
  • Shallots
  • Garlic
  • Olive oil
  • Garam massala
  • Additional spices:
    • Cumin
    • Coriander
    • Smoked paprika
    • Salt
  • Coconut Milk
  • Diced tomatoes
  • Cilantro

Steps:

  1. Cook the rice
  2. Prep the eggplant
  3. Cook the shallots and garlic
  4. Stir the seasonings
  5. Add the milk & tomatoes
  6. Serve with rice and cilantro
